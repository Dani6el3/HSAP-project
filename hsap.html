About HSAP

Harvesters Skill Acquisition Program (HSAP) is an initiative of Harvesters International Christian Center (HICC). It was launched in July 2016 as a response of the unemployment probelm prevalent amongst the Nigerian youth. Our goal is to empower thye young NIgeria population with easy to start, viable and scalable business skills so they can fend for themselves, craete enterprise that can employ others and ultimately contribute to the development of the NIgerian economy.
